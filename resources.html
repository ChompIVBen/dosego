Some people should (safely) continue methadone, some people should (safely) get off of it. It depends on where you are on that spectrum.
While traveling, its hard to remain ancored in a community to recieve atiquate support. If you are needing support, please see the resources below:

  • 12-Step Programs (AA / NA): Open, judgment-free meetings available in most cities; free to attend, no sign-up or commitment required—you can go to any meeting and simply listen.
  • Recovery.com www.recovery.com
  • In crisis right now? Call or Text 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline)
  • Looking for help with addiction or treatment options? → SAMHSA Helpline (1-800-662-HELP)
