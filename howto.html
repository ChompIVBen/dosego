dosego.org - Guest Dosing Instructions

1. Make a Home-Clinic Appointment (ASAP)

  • Bring your dosego.org itinerary to staff (printed or on your phone) and request guest dosing arrangment.
  • Ask: “Do you need any extra forms or signatures? Will you contact the clinics on my route? Will I need to pay anything?”
  • Get the name and phone number of the staff handling it.
  • Ask for a copy of every form (for your travel packet).
  • If home clinic collects payment, get a receipt.

Plan to do this 3–4 weeks ahead of travel.

2. Guest Dosing Confirmation Packet (from your home clinic)

  • Put together a documents packet containing at minimum:
    • Confirmation that home clinic authorized guest dosing.
    • List of guest clinics with dates approved.
    • Contact name & phone at each guest clinic (if available).
  • Confirm Clinic Hours:
    • Call each guest clinic to confirm dosing start/stop times (many stop by late morning). Also ask about holidays/weekends.
    • Check any special instructions (open hours, rules, clinic-specific forms, etc.).

Reccomended: Get both a paper copy and a digital copy on your phone.

3. Prepare Your Travel Packet

  • Government photo ID
  • Guest Dosing packet (from step 2 above)
  • Payment method (cash/credit/debit)
  • Small notebook to record dosing date/time and staff name

4. At the Guest Clinic

Arrive 15–30 minutes early.

  • Show the front desk your paperwork.
  • If asked to sign any extra forms, request a receipt and note the staff name.
  • Record the exact time you received the dose and the staff member’s name in your notebook.

⚠️If Plans Change or You Miss a Date

Call your home clinic first. Then call the guest clinic if home clinic instructs.

⚠️If you feel unwell after dosing, contact the clinic or seek urgent care if it’s an emergency.

✅After the Trip: Let home clinic know you’re back (call or stop by).
