dosego.org - Instructions

1. Make a Home-Clinic Appointment (ASAP)

Bring your itinerary (printed or on your phone). If possible, make this visit 1–4 weeks before travel.

  • Say to staff: “I have a travel itinerary and need guest dosing arranged. Can we start the guest dosing request now?”
  • Ask: “Do you need any forms? Will you contact the clinics on my route? Will I need to pay anything?”
  • Get the name and phone of the staff handling it and sign required consent/forms.
  • Ask for a copy of every signed form for your travel packet.
  • Ask: “Will I pay my home clinic, or will the guest clinics charge me?”
  • If guest clinics collect payment, ask how much per day/dose and which payment methods they accept.
  • If home clinic collects payment, get a receipt.

2. Guest Dosing Confirmation Packet (from your home clinic)

Request a final packet containing at minimum:

  • Confirmation that home clinic authorized guest dosing.
  • List of guest clinics that accepted, with dates/times approved.
  • Contact name & phone at each guest clinic (if available).
  • Any special instructions (arrival time, urine screen rules, etc.).

Reccomended: Get both a paper copy and a digital copy on your phone.
