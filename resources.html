dosego.org - Resources

  • Treatment Assistance: For information on addiction treatment and referral services, contact the SAMHSA National Helpline at (1-800-662-HELP (4357))

  • Crisis Support: Individuals in immediate distress may call or text 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline) for confidential support. If you are in immediate danger, contact local emergency services.

  • Narcotics Anonymous (NA) 12-Step Programs: Open, judgment-free meetings are available in many cities and online. Attendance is free, and no registration or ongoing commitment is required. Individuals may attend any meeting and participate at their discretion. https://na.org/meetingsearch

  • Recovery.com: www.recovery.com
